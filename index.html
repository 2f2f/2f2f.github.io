Frequently Asked Questions

I can just hire a developer.Why this site?

The purpose of starting this site was to bridge the gap between users who need a full-fledged website/app and casual users who need a quick solution to everyday problems but do not have the technical know-how to do the modifications themselves.

So you dont make websites or apps ?

Thats correct,we only make modifications for existing websites.

Is modifying websites legal ?

We do not modify the orignial in any way. It's all client-side.So only you will be able to view the altered version. For everyone else,it will look the same. Also when it comes to MMORPG's,we dont provide mods if against ToS.

Ok great.How much do you charge ?

We charge absolutely NOTHING for small mods.A general interest in technology promted us to make this site.Besides many great tools like tampermonkey and Stylish which are used for mods are free,please support the creators of these add-ins.However for time-consuming mods(and VBA),we may charge a nominal fee. Please contact us.
